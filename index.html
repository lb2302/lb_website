Company Profile

Laundry Boy was founded by Mr. Mohd Amjad Niyazi through self-funded initiatives. We have established our flagship state-of-the-art Dry Clean/Laundry Lab in the heart of Mumbai, Andheri West, Oshiwara and Versova , with an initial investment of Rs. 20 lakhs. Our strategic vision includes further investment of Rs. 1 Crore over the next 12 months to open ten new outlets in every 2 Km radius. Our expansion plans span 12, 24, and 36 months, aimed at establishing our brand presence across Mumbai and in India. Our primary objective is to provide substantial returns to our investors over the long term. We continuously research and implement various types of detergent chemicals and machinery to optimize our operations for cost-effectiveness. As part of our mission to make laundry services accessible to every Indian, we prioritize cost efficiency in our day-to-day operations. This approach ensures that while our customers benefit from affordable services, our investors also enjoy increased profits from their investments.
